Purpose

Educate users about the common hazards at the beach and how to stay safe.

General Beach Safety Tips

Guidelines for swimming safely, using flotation devices, and identifying safe swimming areas.

Example: "Always swim in areas with lifeguards and heed their advice."

Common Hazards

Information on rip currents, jellyfish, sunburn, dehydration, etc.

Prevention tips and how to deal with these hazards:

  • Rip Currents: Know how to identify and escape rip currents.
  • Jellyfish: Avoid swimming in areas where jellyfish are known to be present.
  • Sunburn: Always use sunscreen with a high SPF and reapply regularly.
  • Dehydration: Drink plenty of water, especially in hot weather.

Emergency Situations

What to do in case of an emergency:

  • Call lifeguards for assistance.
  • Provide basic first aid if trained to do so.

Emergency Contact Numbers:

Make sure to have the following numbers handy:

  • Lifeguard Station: 123-456-7890
  • Local Emergency Services: 911
