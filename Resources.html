FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about beach safety and conservation:

  • Q: What should I do if I get caught in a rip current?
    A: Swim parallel to the shore until you are free from the current, then swim back to the beach.
  • Q: How can I help with beach conservation?
    A: Participate in local cleanup events and reduce plastic usage.
  • Q: What are the signs of dehydration?
    A: Symptoms include thirst, dry mouth, fatigue, and dark-colored urine.
